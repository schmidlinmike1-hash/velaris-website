Admirals — Velaris: Galactic Strategy

12 unique admirals — three per faction. Select a portrait to load the dossier into the terminal.

Independent · Legendary

Valdris — The Unbroken

The only admiral to survive the Convergence Catastrophe with an intact fleet. No one knows how. He does not speak of it. Since then he has shaped every major faction crisis — always on the side he believes to be right, not the strongest. That alone is legendary.

Independent · Legendary

Xel'Nara — The Divided

Half Nythera, half Aurelian — treated as an outsider by both cultures, shaped by both. She has no faction because she understands them all. Teams under her command never make mistakes born of panic. No one understands why.

Aurelian Concordance · Epic

Thorn Aegis — The Unbreakable

A veteran of the silent wars and one of the last living souls who experienced the Fragmentation Crisis first-hand. His scars — ceremonial and combat alike — make him a walking history of the Concordance. Teaches tactics these days. Is not contradicted.

Aurelian Concordance · Rare

Lyra Voss — Commander of the Convergence

Raised aboard the flagship of the Convergence fleet. Assigned to the fleet at 19, commanding her own cruiser by 34. Known as the coldest person in the room in moments of crisis — not out of callousness, but out of absolute discipline.

Aurelian Concordance · Uncommon

Kael Dorn — The Foresighted

Started in the archive branch and transferred to the fleet at 25 — the loss of status spurred him on instead of slowing him down. His reputation: plans every move three steps further than necessary.

Nythera Collective · Epic

Weise Syn'Ara — Keeper of Memory

Her bioluminescence glows constantly, even without neural activity — a phenomenon Nythera science cannot explain. She never comments on it. Regarded as the bridge between the old collective memory and the new generation.

Nythera Collective · Rare

Archivist Vel'Oran — Voice of the Homeworld

Experienced the Helios Event as a youngling and is the only living link to the complete data imprint of the homeworld. Rarely speaks — when he does, the entire Collective listens.

Nythera Collective · Uncommon

Analyst Seyr — The Observer

Freshly linked, yet with one of the densest data contributions of any young Nythera in generations. Observes everything, says little.

Khar'Vex Dominion · Epic

Kommandantin Gr'Vath — Tournament Champion

The only Khar'Vex to compete in all four clan tournaments of the past decade — and win three of them. Respected, feared, and secretly found fascinating by the Nythera.

Khar'Vex Dominion · Rare

Warlord Kex'Daran — Leader of the Oldest Clan

Leader of the oldest of the seven clans. Has won more cross-clan duels than anyone in two generations — and lost more. "Those who cannot lose have never truly fought."

Khar'Vex Dominion · Uncommon

Krieger Doh'Karas — The Impatient

A young Khar'Vex fresh from his trial of proving. Few pins yet, but a name you remember. Impatient, direct, loyal to the bone.

Virellian Consortia · Epic

Gildemeisterin Ysa — Mistress of the Routes

Most trade routes in the Velaris sector pass through at least one contract she signed at some point. Nobody knows how old she is.

Virellian Consortia · Rare

Händlerin Oryn — Arbiter of Route Law

A specialist in route law — knows every clause, every precedent. Other factions bring her in as a neutral arbiter in trade disputes.

Virellian Consortia · Uncommon

Makler Tavi — The Broker

Negotiated his first route between Aurelians and Khar'Vex at 22 — and made both sides feel like they had won. Today he has contacts in all four factions.
