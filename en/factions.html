Choose Your Faction — Velaris: Galactic Strategy

Five powers shape the sector. Select a system on the map to pull up the faction profile.

Stability · Unity · Structure

Aurelian Concordance

Born from a coalition of interstellar generation ships fleeing the catastrophic Helios Event. Stranded far from their origin, the surviving fleets united under a strict coordination doctrine. Their modern philosophy: structure is survival, order is strength.

Efficiency · Analysis · Optimization

Nythera Collective

Evolved on a planet dominated by electromagnetic storms and developed a neuro-resonant network for near-instant data exchange. For the Nythera, territory is irrelevant — information density defines value.

Strength · Dominance · Superiority

Khar'Vex Dominion

Forged on a high-gravity world where only extreme physical endurance ensured survival. The "War of Seven Circles" united their world under a single warlord. To them, strength is not cruelty — it is proof of fitness.

Trade · Logistics · Influence

Virellian Consortia

Originate from a mineral-poor world where survival depended on cooperation. Their civilization is structured around trade guilds and resource syndicates. For them, controlling the flow is superior to controlling territory.

Bio-mechanical swarm · NPC threat

Xal'Thyrr Swarm

Not a civilization, but an adaptive bio-mechanical swarm consciousness of unknown origin. Reacts aggressively to high technological activity — serving as pressure that forces alliances and prevents stagnation.
