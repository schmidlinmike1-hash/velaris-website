1. Introduction

Welcome to Velaris: Galactic Strategy ("the Game"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

Local Data Only: Velaris: Galactic Strategy is primarily an offline game. All game progress, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device using standard device storage mechanisms.

The Game may collect the following types of information:

  • Game Progress: Building levels, fleet compositions, research status, resources, achievements, and other gameplay data — stored locally on your device.
  • Preferences: Language selection, display settings, and notification preferences — stored locally on your device.
  • Device Information: Basic device type and operating system version for compatibility purposes.

3. How We Use Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the Game's functionality
  • To save and restore your game progress
  • To remember your language and display preferences
  • To improve the Game's performance and user experience

4. Data Storage and Security

Your game data is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit your personal gameplay data to external servers unless you explicitly use features that require server communication (such as alliance features or cloud sync, if available). We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your data.

5. Third-Party Services

The Game may use the following third-party services:

  • Expo / React Native: The application framework used to build the Game.
  • Google Play Services: For app distribution on Android devices.

These services may collect certain technical data as described in their respective privacy policies.

6. Children's Privacy

The Game is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

7. In-App Purchases

The Game may offer in-app purchases. All payment processing is handled by the respective app store (Google Play Store). We do not collect or store any payment information.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your game data (stored locally on your device)
  • Delete your game data by uninstalling the Game or clearing app data
  • Change your preferences at any time within the Game's settings

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the Game or on this page. Changes are effective immediately upon posting.
